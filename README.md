Your AI-powered productivity coach that transforms dreams into daily actions.

- **Smart Onboarding** - 6-step productivity assessment
- **Voice-to-Task** - Speak your tasks (UI ready for AI integration)
- **AI Productivity Coach** - Expert coaching chat (simulated for MVP)
